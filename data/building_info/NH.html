Originally known as the Elgin Watch Factory, Nebraska Hall is a massive building that has been remodeled several times over and features more than 320,000 square feet of gross space.

Nebraska Hall houses the departments of Construction Management, Civil Engineering, Engineering Mechanics, Industrial and Management Systems Engineering, and the Engineering Library. It is physically linked with the Walter Scott Engineering Center and provides both laboratory, classroom, and office space for the wide range of specific areas within Engineering.

The third floor of Nebraska Hall also houses the University Communications Publications department, while the west side of the fourth and fifth floors house the Nebraska State Museum's Systematics Research Collections.
